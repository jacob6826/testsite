About

I am a researcher obsessed with the "Why" behind user behavior. With a background bridging safety-critical aerospace environments and high-velocity consumer products, I bring a unique perspective to User Experience.

My approach combines the rigor of Human Factors engineering, learned while working on NASA crew displays, with the agility of modern product discovery. Whether reducing error rates in a lunar lander simulation or streamlining the live-betting flow for millions of users, my goal remains the same: designing intuitive experiences grounded in cognitive psychology.
